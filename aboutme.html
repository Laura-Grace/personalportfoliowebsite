About Me

I grew up in Cheshire, before beginning my undergraduate at the University of Buckingham; I graduated with a 2:1 in History and English. Around this time, the pandemic began and I decided to continue studying and gained a PGCE in Primary Education with a focus on Special Needs. However, I realised in my first year of work as a teacher that I passionately valued the ability to be creative, problem-solve and grow through development. Unfortunately, this meant that teaching was no longer the career for me as it didn't align with these values. I decided to take some time out of work to pursue various passions of mine, and quickly found myself enamoured by graphic design. I took various courses on Adobe and began to grow my skill set as I used this knowledge to aid in a company I founded. My company also needed a website, and this is how I came to discover front-end development. I have pushed myself to work consistently and with dedication; I am now proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitBash and GitHub. I am working on growing these skills further and becoming more well-rounded in a variety of programming languages.
